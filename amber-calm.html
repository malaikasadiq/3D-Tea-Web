LUXTEA Amber Calm Cinnamon black tea

Taste profile

Golden spice with a soft, clear finish.

Amber Calm opens with malty black tea, moves into cinnamon warmth, then settles into green cardamom and orange peel. The mint is subtle: it lifts the final sip without turning the cup cold.

  • Malt and honey at the base
  • Cinnamon bark and cardamom through the middle
  • Orange peel brightness and a clean mint finish

Ingredients

Whole ingredients you can see.

Loose black tea, cinnamon bark, cardamom pods, orange peel, mint leaf, and a small touch of vanilla-style natural aroma for roundness.

Black tea Cinnamon Cardamom Orange peel Mint

Brew guide

How to make Amber Calm taste luxurious.

01Preheat

Warm the cup or pot for 20 seconds so the spice blooms smoothly.

02Measure

Use 2.5 grams of loose tea for each 8 ounces of water.

03Steep

Pour 203 F water and steep for 4 minutes. Strain fully.

04Finish

Drink clear first, then add warm milk if you want a softer evening cup.

Best moment

Late afternoon, quiet desk, low lamp, and the first pause after a long run of work.

Pair with

Butter cookies, toasted brioche, oat milk, almond cake, or dark chocolate.

Serve as

A clear spiced black tea, a small milk tea, or a concentrated latte base.
