LUXTEA Rose Night Floral herbal cup

Taste profile

Soft floral aroma, apple body, gentle finish.

Rose Night is floral but not perfumed. Rose and lavender rise first, chamomile brings honeyed softness, apple adds body, and tulsi keeps the blend grounded.

  • Rose, lavender, and chamomile aroma
  • Apple sweetness through the center
  • Tulsi and meadow herb finish

Ingredients

Botanicals for a slower evening.

Rose petals, chamomile blossoms, lavender, apple pieces, tulsi, lemon balm, and a small amount of hibiscus for color.

Rose Chamomile Lavender Apple Tulsi

Brew guide

How to keep Rose Night soft and fragrant.

01Use a glass pot

The petals and blossoms open beautifully and color the water slowly.

02Measure generously

Use 3 grams for each 8 ounces for a full floral body.

03Steep longer

Six minutes gives chamomile and apple enough time to round the cup.

04Serve plain

Drink without milk. A small spoon of honey works if you want extra softness.

Best moment

Night reading, a bath, a slow journal page, or a phone-free hour before sleep.

Pair with

Honey, almond biscuits, poached pear, shortbread, and quiet music.

Serve as

A clear floral cup, a honeyed nightcap, or a chilled botanical infusion.
