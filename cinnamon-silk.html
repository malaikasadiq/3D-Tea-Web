LUXTEA Cinnamon Silk Spiced vanilla tea

Taste profile

Velvet spice with cocoa depth.

Cinnamon Silk is richer and rounder than Amber Calm. Cacao shell adds a dark dessert note, chicory adds roasted depth, and vanilla bean gives the finish its soft texture.

  • Sweet cinnamon and vanilla opening
  • Black tea and toasted chicory body
  • Cocoa shell and cream-like finish

Ingredients

Comforting, visible, and slow blooming.

Black tea, cinnamon bark, vanilla bean, cacao shell, toasted chicory, and a small touch of orange peel.

Black tea Cinnamon Vanilla Cacao shell Chicory

Brew guide

How to make Cinnamon Silk feel creamy.

01Use enough leaf

Use 3 grams for each 8 ounces when making a milk tea.

02Steep hot

203 F water unlocks the cinnamon bark and cacao shell.

03Give it time

Five minutes builds the smooth body this blend is known for.

04Add milk

Warm milk or oat cream turns it into a dessert-like cup.

Best moment

Morning with pastry, dessert after dinner, or a weekend cup that replaces coffee.

Pair with

Dark chocolate, cinnamon rolls, almond biscotti, pears, and oat milk.

Serve as

A rich milk tea, latte base, or long steeped dessert cup.
