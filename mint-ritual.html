Taste profile

Bright mint, soft herb, clean finish.

Mint Ritual is crisp without becoming sharp. Spearmint leads, lemon verbena adds citrus lift, fennel rounds the cup, and black tea tips give enough body to make it feel complete.

  • Cooling mint and fresh meadow aroma
  • Light citrus through the middle
  • Soft fennel sweetness at the finish

Ingredients

Clean herbs with a tea backbone.

Spearmint, black tea tips, lemon verbena, nettle leaf, fennel seed, and a touch of citrus peel.

Spearmint Tea tips Lemon verbena Nettle Fennel

Brew guide

How to keep Mint Ritual clean and elegant.

01Use cooler water

195 F keeps the mint bright and protects the delicate verbena.

02Measure lightly

Use 2 grams for each 8 ounces if drinking clear.

03Steep briefly

Three minutes is enough for aroma, body, and color.

04Serve fresh

Drink hot, or pour over ice with lemon for a clear afternoon cup.

Best moment

After lunch, before a meeting, or whenever the room needs air and focus.

Pair with

Lemon cake, cucumber toast, fruit, light salads, and honey.

Serve as

A clear cup, iced mint tea, or sparkling cold brew with citrus.
